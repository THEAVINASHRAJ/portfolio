ScanPay · Fintech · 2024–Present

On the way to hit $1Bn TPV — without a big budget or a big team.

When Avinash joined ScanPay, the home services payments market was saturated. Every competitor had years of head start and bigger budgets. Instead of chasing them, he changed the game. He identified that contractors in Facebook groups and trade communities were already talking about payment pain — but no brand was listening or showing up as a peer. ScanPay's sales reps started joining those spaces, not as salespeople, but as members who understood the job. Meanwhile, a bold pricing model — no monthly fees and lowest transaction fee — removed the biggest friction in the sales conversation. Month-over-month customer growth hit 10% on average. Marketing cost per acquisition dropped by 33%.
