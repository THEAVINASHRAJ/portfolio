ScanPay · Fintech 2024—Now
ScanPay

On the way to $1Bn TPV, without a big budget or a big team.

When Avinash joined ScanPay, the home services payments market was saturated. Every competitor had years of head start and bigger budgets. Instead of chasing them, he changed the game. He saw that contractors in Facebook groups and trade communities were already talking about payment pain, but no brand was listening or showing up as a peer. ScanPay's reps started joining those spaces as members who understood the job, not as salespeople. A bold pricing model, with no monthly fees and the lowest transaction fee, removed the biggest friction in the sales conversation. Month-over-month merchant growth hit 20% on average, and acquisition cost dropped by a third.

04 Builds With AI

Computer science engineer by training. Avinash uses AI to build and ship real products solo, not mockups, but live tools the team actually uses.

🤖
ScanPay Sales Kit — an AI agent inside Slack
Sales reps were losing hours to two non-selling tasks: hunting for product answers mid-call, and waiting a day or two on marketing for on-brand outreach. Avinash built an AI agent that lives in Slack, giving instant product answers with help-center links, plus on-brand content generation with translate and save. Built solo with Python on the Slack Bolt SDK, the OpenAI API for retrieval and generation, brand guidelines injected into every call, version-controlled on GitHub, and deployed on Railway for around-the-clock uptime. Content turnaround dropped from one or two days to under a minute.
